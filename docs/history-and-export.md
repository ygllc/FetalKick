# History and export

Fetal Kick keeps a record of your saved sessions.

### Review your sessions

Use the history view to check:

* sessions for a specific day
* your daily totals
* patterns over time

The calendar helps you spot gaps and compare days quickly.

### Export your data

{% hint style="warning" %}
Export and Settings are currently in progress, so you won't see them yet
{% endhint %}

Export creates a `CSV` file with your tracking data.

This is useful when you want to:

* keep a backup
* review trends in a spreadsheet
* share records with your care team

### Before you export

Check that your recent sessions are saved.

A session only saves when it has at least one recorded kick.

{% hint style="info" %}
Export is for convenience. It does not replace medical advice.
{% endhint %}
